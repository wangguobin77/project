About SENSEPLAY

SENSEPLAY team have wild imaginations about this world. In our imagined world, we can turn into Gulliver, running at the ground level, to rediscover familiar yet fresh scenes in our life and surrounding nature. We can hover in the sky, overseeing the beautiful landscape wide and far. We can also dive into the deep blue to explore the oceanic wonders under the wave. Also, we believe we can do something, to connect the real environment with the virtual world and to empower our peer players with the capabilities to navigate through mixed reality.

After numurous setbacks, twists and frustrations, we eventually put together the first generation of SENSEPLAY FPV product series for players. We expect this groundbreaking "new species" to equip players with "new vision", allowing them to see a different world.

SENSEPLAY team is driven by a genuine passion to innovate and will continue to present "new species" products. We call for players as curious and enthusiastic as you, to join hands with us and create endless possibilities of seeing an FPV world.

SENSEPLAY is

a brand-new FPV world
that empowers devices with unparalleled FPV capabilities and offers fresh visions to this world;
a video streaming and remote control system
that connects FPV devices over an ultra-long distance;
an AR gaming platform
that allows developers to build next-gen AR games on FPV devices;
a technology and product innovation alliance
that combines the strengths of hardware makers and AR game developers;
a united marketplace
that enables all partners to cross-promote their products and generate new revenue streams
