SENSEPLAY dazzles audience at CES Asia 2018

June 13-15, 2018, Sensethink Technology staged a live show of its latest SENSEPLAYFPV remote control car series for...
by SENSEPLAY Jun 15, 2018

June 13-15, 2018, Sensethink Technology staged a live show of its latest SENSEPLAYFPV remote control car series for visitors during the 3 days of CES Asia 2018, where the world’s hottest and most leading products and technologies are presented from the areas of artificial intelligence, 5G, self-driving cars, etc. As a product platform that empowers consumer electronic devices with first-person-view (FPV) capability as well as augmented reality (AR) gaming features, SENSEPLAY stood out from a variety of innovative products at the show and won the first place in the “Last Gadget Standing” competition (https://asia.lastgadgetstanding.com/) on the opening day.
(SENSEPLAY Booth at CES Asia 2018 (Hall N3, 3236))
(Glory on the opening day)

Sensethink Technology has taken a leading position in the fields of wireless remote control and real-time high-definition video transmission. The SENSEPLAYFPV RC car series, live demonstrated to audience during this show, has unique features:

  • A smart wireless remote communication module that enables real-time 1080P@30fps video streaming over a maximum of 1km near-ground remote control distance. The module works on both 2.4GHz and 5.8GHz frequency, delivers 10Mbps adaptive bandwidth transmission rate, and boasts high reception sensitivity and strong anti-interference ability;

  • Brushless DC motor with magnetic encoder adjustment to ensure precise control and powerful torque;

  • Front and back HD starlight cameras, bringing clear night vision; 130 degree ultra wide FOV；

  • Front and back HD starlight cameras, bringing clear night vision; 130 degree ultra wide FOV；

  • High-performance 10000mAh li-polymer battery pack supporting 2 hour continuous working, with smart BMS including digital volume display.


(An insider’s look at SENSEPLAY FPV RC cars)

SENSEPLAY booth and its 12-meter runway has been crowded by enthusiastic visitors who had first-hand experience with this innovative and interesting product throughout the show. Visitors, including many seasoned RC car fans, applauded the real-time HD image quality and powerful battery endurance of these FPV cars.

SENSEPLAY FPV RC car series will be available for pre-sale at online channels in July. The positive feedback from CES Asia visitors substantiates the huge market potential for FPV-enabled products. As a premier FPV product platform,

SENSEPLAY is committed in exploring the technology breakthroughs and innovations and connecting the communities of hardware producers and software developers.

About Senseplay

SENSEPLAY is a product platform that empowers consumer electronic devices with first-person-view (FPV) capability as well as augmented reality (AR) gaming features. The core components of the SENSEPLAY platform include a series of universal remote controls, a standardized FPV module and a ready-to-use software development kit (SDK). Facilitated by all these components, SENSEPLAY fosters a community where hardware producers, game developers and consumer players can connect and interact. For more information, please visit www.senseplay.com.
